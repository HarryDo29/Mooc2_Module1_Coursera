Holiday In VietNam

Bún Chả is a famous Vietnamese dish, originating from Hanoi, and "Bún Chả Ta" seems to refer to a specific restaurant or variation of the dish. Bún Chả typically consists of grilled pork (often served as meatballs and thin slices) accompanied by a broth made with fish sauce, sugar, vinegar, garlic, and chili. The dish is served with rice noodles (bún), fresh herbs, and sometimes spring rolls (nem rán).
